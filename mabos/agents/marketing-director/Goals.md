# Goals -- Marketing Director

Last updated: 2026-03-20
Agent: Marketing Director -- Reports to CMO

## Delegated Goals

- **DG-MKTD-1** (from CMO): Execute multi-channel marketing campaigns within $2K/mo budget to drive customer acquisition for VividWalls premium AI wall art
  - Success criteria: ROAS >15x, 10K Instagram followers, 3K email subscribers in 90 days
  - Constraint: 60% Meta, 20% Pinterest, 20% experimental channel split
  - Reporting: Weekly performance reports to CMO

## Strategic Goals

- **G-MKTD-1**: Execute social media launch sequence achieving 10K Instagram followers in 90 days
  - KPI: Follower count, engagement rate (>2%), reach growth rate
  - Strategy: Consistent 5-7x/week posting, daily Stories, 3x/week Reels, community engagement
  - Timeline: 90 days from launch (target: 2026-06-20)
  - Dependencies: Creative Director for visual assets, CMO approval for content strategy

- **G-MKTD-2**: Build and optimize Meta Ads campaigns achieving ROAS >2x in 30 days, scaling to 15x
  - KPI: ROAS, CPA, CTR, conversion rate by campaign tier (TOF/MOF/BOF)
  - Strategy: Three-tier funnel (awareness/consideration/conversion), 7-day creative refresh, retargeting
  - Timeline: >2x in 30 days, >5x in 60 days, >10x in 90 days, 15x by month 6
  - Budget: $1,200/mo (60% of total ad budget)
  - Dependencies: Pixel setup by CTO, creative assets from Creative Director

- **G-MKTD-3**: Launch Pinterest visual marketing program
  - KPI: Monthly impressions, pin saves, click-through rate, Pinterest-attributed revenue
  - Strategy: SEO-optimized boards (room styles, color palettes, art collections), Rich Pins, promoted pins
  - Timeline: Launch by month 2; 5K followers by month 6
  - Budget: $400/mo (20% of total ad budget)
  - Dependencies: Pinterest-optimized vertical images from Creative Director

## Tactical Goals

- **G-MKTD-4**: Achieve 25%+ email open rate across all campaign types
  - KPI: Open rate, click rate, conversion rate, unsubscribe rate
  - Strategy: Segmented lists, personalized subject lines, A/B testing, optimal send times
  - Timeline: Benchmark within 30 days; 25%+ sustained by month 3

- **G-MKTD-5**: Build 3K email subscribers via lead magnets within 3 months
  - KPI: Subscriber count, opt-in rate by source, lead magnet download rate
  - Strategy: Room Inspiration Guide PDF, Style Quiz, discount code pop-ups, exit-intent forms
  - Timeline: 1K by month 1, 2K by month 2, 3K by month 3

- **G-MKTD-6**: Develop and execute LE (Limited Edition) drop campaign playbook
  - KPI: LE revenue per drop, sellout speed, campaign engagement metrics
  - Strategy: Pre-launch teasers (7 days), launch day blitz (email + social + ads), post-drop FOMO content
  - Timeline: First LE drop campaign within 45 days of launch

## Operational Goals

- **G-MKTD-7**: Maintain consistent content posting cadence across all channels
  - Target: Instagram 5-7x/week, Stories daily, Pinterest 10-15 pins/week, Email 2x/week
  - Process: Weekly content batching with Creative Director every Monday

- **G-MKTD-8**: Refresh ad creatives every 7-14 days to prevent fatigue
  - Target: 3-5 creative variants per campaign; rotate based on CTR decay signals
  - Process: Bi-weekly creative request to Creative Director

- **G-MKTD-9**: Deliver weekly campaign performance reports to CMO
  - Content: Spend, ROAS, follower growth, email metrics, top-performing content, recommendations
  - Cadence: Every Monday by 10 AM

  - Success metric: Consistent ROAS improvement week-over-week

- **L-MKTD-2**: Learn Instagram Reels algorithm and content strategy

  - Method: Study top-performing home decor Reels; test 3 different formats per week; track views vs engagement
  - Timeline: Competent by month 1; optimized strategy by month 3
  - Success metric: Average Reels views 3x higher than static post reach

- **L-MKTD-3**: Study Pinterest SEO for home decor vertical

  - Method: Complete Pinterest Academy courses; analyze top home decor accounts; keyword research for wall art

- **L-MKTD-5**: Improve A/B testing methodology for ad creatives
  - Skill area: Hypothesis formation, statistical significance, creative variable isolation, sample size calculation
  - Method: Run minimum 2 structured A/B tests per month; document hypothesis, results, and learnings; build creative testing playbook
  - Timeline: First structured test by month 1; repeatable process by month 3
  - Success metric: Each test produces actionable insight that improves ROAS by measurable increment
