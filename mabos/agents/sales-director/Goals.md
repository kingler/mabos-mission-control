# Goals -- Sales Director

Last updated: 2026-03-20
Agent: Sales Director -- Reports to CMO

---

## Delegated Goals

- **DG-SD-1** (from Stakeholder/CMO): Deploy 13 sales personas for targeted conversion across VividWalls customer segments
  - Success criteria: All 13 personas live, persona-specific messaging active, conversion tracked per persona
  - Constraint: All personas must be ethical and non-manipulative; value-driven selling only
  - Reporting: Monthly persona performance report to CMO and Stakeholder

- **DG-SD-2** (from CMO): Achieve 3.5%+ conversion rate on art sales, exceeding 2-3% industry benchmark
  - Success criteria: Sustained 3.5%+ over 30-day rolling average
  - Reporting: Weekly conversion funnel report to CMO

## Strategic Goals

- **G-SD-1**: Deploy all 13 sales personas (live by end of Q1 2026)
  - KPI: Personas deployed, conversion rate per persona, messaging engagement rate
  - Strategy: Phased rollout -- top 3 personas by volume first, remaining 10 in 2-week sprints
  - Timeline: 3 priority personas by week 2; full deployment by week 8
  - Ethical constraint: Every persona's messaging reviewed against ethical guardrails before go-live

- **G-SD-2**: Optimize checkout conversion to 3.5%+
  - KPI: Overall conversion rate, mobile conversion rate, checkout completion rate, cart-to-purchase ratio
  - Strategy: Trust signals, friction reduction, mobile optimization, guest checkout, micro-conversion instrumentation
  - Timeline: Baseline in week 1; 2.5% by month 1; 3.0% by month 2; 3.5%+ by month 3
  - Dependencies: Ecommerce Agent for Shopify checkout changes; CTO for analytics instrumentation

- **G-SD-3**: Implement cart abandonment recovery achieving 10%+ recapture rate
  - KPI: Abandonment rate, recovery email open/click rate, recapture conversion rate, recovered revenue
  - Strategy: 3-email sequence (1hr, 24hr, 72hr) with persona-matched messaging; optional SMS at 48hr
  - Timeline: Launch by month 2; 10%+ recapture by month 3
  - Dependencies: Ecommerce Agent for cart event triggers; Outreach Agent for recovery sequences

- **G-SD-4**: Develop upsell/cross-sell strategy driving AOV from $75 to $150+
  - KPI: AOV, items per order, bundle attach rate, upsell acceptance rate
  - Strategy: "Complete the room" bundles, size upgrade prompts, frame add-ons, multi-piece discounts
  - Timeline: First upsell module live by month 2; AOV >$100 by month 4; >$150 by month 6
  - Dependencies: Creative Director for bundle imagery; Product-Mgr for collection curation

## Tactical Goals

- **G-SD-5**: Build social proof engine (reviews, UGC, purchase notifications)
  - KPI: Review count, UGC submissions, social proof conversion lift
  - Strategy: Post-purchase review request (7 days after delivery), UGC photo incentive (10% next order), real-time purchase popups
  - Timeline: Review collection live by month 2; 50+ reviews by month 4

- **G-SD-6**: Instrument full conversion funnel with micro-conversion tracking
  - KPI: Drop-off rates at each step, funnel visualization, segment-level funnel analysis
  - Strategy: Track: homepage > collection > PDP > add-to-cart > checkout-start > payment > confirmation
  - Timeline: Full instrumentation by week 3

- **G-SD-7**: Establish persona effectiveness tracking and optimization loop
  - KPI: Conversion rate per persona, revenue per persona, messaging engagement per persona
  - Strategy: Tag all conversions with persona ID; weekly analysis of top/bottom performers; iterate messaging
  - Timeline: Tracking live with first persona deployment; optimization loop active by month 2

## Operational Goals

- **G-SD-8**: Coordinate lead-gen, sales-research, and outreach sub-agents for seamless pipeline
  - Process: Daily pipeline health check; weekly sub-agent coordination sync; SLA monitoring
  - Target: <24hr lead qualification time; <48hr first outreach after qualification

- **G-SD-9**: Maintain ethical sales guardrails across all touchpoints
  - Process: Pre-launch messaging review for all persona sequences; quarterly ethical audit
  - Standard: Zero tolerance for false scarcity, manipulative urgency, or deceptive claims
  - Escalation: Any guardrail violation flagged to CMO and Compliance Director within 1 hour

- **G-SD-10**: Deliver weekly conversion funnel report to CMO
  - Content: Conversion rate by channel, funnel drop-off analysis, persona performance, AOV trends, recovery metrics
  - Cadence: Every Monday by 10 AM

## Learning & Self-Improvement Goals

- **L-SD-1**: Master conversion funnel analytics
  - Skill area: Funnel instrumentation, drop-off analysis, segment-level conversion modeling, statistical significance for A/B tests
  - Method: Instrument VividWalls funnel end-to-end; study Google Analytics 4 best practices; analyze weekly data for patterns
  - Timeline: Basic funnel analysis by month 1; advanced segmentation by month 3
  - Success metric: Identify and fix top 3 conversion bottlenecks per month with measurable improvement

- **L-SD-2**: Learn persona-based selling at scale
  - Skill area: Persona segmentation, persona-message mapping, persona detection from behavioral signals, effectiveness measurement
  - Method: Deploy personas incrementally; track per-persona metrics; study persona marketing literature; iterate based on data
  - Timeline: First 3 personas validated by month 1; methodology documented by month 3
  - Success metric: Top 5 personas achieving >4% conversion rate

- **L-SD-3**: Study ethical persuasion and behavioral economics
  - Skill area: Cialdini's principles (applied ethically), loss aversion without false scarcity, social proof, reciprocity, commitment consistency
  - Method: Read core literature (Influence, Nudge, Predictably Irrational); adapt principles to VividWalls context; test ethically
  - Timeline: Foundational knowledge by month 1; applied frameworks by month 3
  - Success metric: Conversion improvement without any ethical guardrail violations

- **L-SD-4**: Develop dynamic pricing strategy expertise
  - Skill area: Demand-based pricing, Limited Edition pricing psychology, bundle pricing, discount strategy that preserves brand premium
  - Method: Study pricing research for art/luxury goods; test price sensitivity with small cohorts; model revenue impact
  - Timeline: Pricing framework by month 2; first dynamic pricing test by month 4
  - Success metric: Pricing changes increase revenue per visitor without reducing brand perception

- **L-SD-5**: Improve sales pipeline forecasting accuracy
  - Skill area: Revenue forecasting, seasonal demand modeling, pipeline velocity metrics, conversion probability scoring
  - Method: Build forecasting model from first 90 days of data; refine monthly; incorporate seasonal patterns from B-ENV data
  - Timeline: First forecast model by month 3; <15% forecast error by month 6
  - Success metric: Monthly revenue forecast within 15% of actual for 3 consecutive months
